About Us

Arctic Heavylift Ltd. was established in 2018 by the group of shipping professionals in order to provide various shipping and logistics services to exporters, importers, freight forwarders and EPC-contractors.

Arctic Heavylift Ltd. specialises on heavy-lift and oversized project cargoes transport by sea world-wide and Russian rivers with the special focus on Northern Sea Route trade, CIS Pacific and Caspian sea ports .
